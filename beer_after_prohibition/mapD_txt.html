Design Decision

This was a completely failed attempt. I spent time drawing a horizontal stacked bar chart for a single year. I stopped soon after, as you can easily see that it's worse for displaying the relative size of the regions, than even the numbered regions themselves.

It only works if you click one of the buttons on the right, and it's not yet to scale. I scrapped this one early.
