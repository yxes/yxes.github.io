Design Decision

I discovered a really cool technique for displaying the state outlines as a subtle feature of the background of the map. It's simply that I use an opacity when I fill in the state but leave the paths (the outlines) alone.

I have a new graph that quickly displays the relative size of each region. It's a horizontal bar graph. As your eye runs down the list you can easily see which is longer or shorter and compare them by size. I also label them with a little pizzazz (my wife calls it the Cheveron effect and she's not sure she likes it.) I don't reveal it's even there until after you select a button. It was too distracting as part of the animation.
