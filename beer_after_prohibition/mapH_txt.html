I'm sure you'll be as amazed as I was, but several people didn't see how the two graphs were related. After my initial moment of frustration and shock, I was asked if I could just make the lower bar flash when you moused over the region. I have no idea... maybe?

Design Decision

Pull the yellow color from the pint and have it change the current color of the region and the bar below. In an effort to ensure that it just "flashes", I used a timeout in combination with an onMouseOut event. Keep in mind that each region is really a series of states so the on mouse over event is tied to the states. If that's confusing - it was for me too.
