I started with nine colors derived from ColorBrewer in the Blues section. I then displayed those colors based on the ranges (using every 1/2 point) from the pints / week values.

Since the regional values are per capita, there's no statistical value to combining them. However, reviewing the overall minimum and maximum regional values might be a novel way to reconsider the trends. This way I can display overall trends in a smaller format. Without making the graph even larger, I opted to use this new format as my input device. Using mouseover effects instead of clicks allowed me to effectively make the trends more relevent.

Design Decision

I ended up removing the buttons, replacing it with mini-line graph which I displayed vertically to allow everything to fit. I overlayed major events in my input section and shaded them between the years to provide more emphasis, rather than just the text on the right-size of the map.
