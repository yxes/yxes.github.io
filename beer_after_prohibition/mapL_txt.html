The colors on this map now need to do two things, they need to not only reveal the region, but they need to conform to the values to reveal the amounts from each value. The problem with choosing one scheme over the other is that you loose the other.

The current design can hide a region if the two values are very close together. This is especially problematic if the two regions are next to each other on the map.

Design Decision

I combined the two formats. First I ranked the regions by value and then plugged in the colors according to those rankings. This ensured that each of the colors show off the region as well as the ranking of the values.
