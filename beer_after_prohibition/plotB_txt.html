The plot really needed a legend. However, putting in a legend required changing the points to different colors.

Design Decision

Most of the time a legend is housed in the upper right of a scatter plot. The reader's eye will generally go there to find the legend.

A more difficult decision was what color schemes to use. I showed several colors to friends and colleagues before deciding that a monochrome color scheme worked best. I would love to say that the choice of red had some deep culutural implications derived from the dataset. However, my wife wanted red and I wasn't in a position to refuse. I ended up using various shades of red on the white background.

VIEW

I would like to point out that red wasn't a good choice here (hopefully my wife won't read this). It turns out that women see many more shades of red than men and since we are trying to compare the different regions, today I would have chosen a different color.
