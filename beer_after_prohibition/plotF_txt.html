Design Decision

I want a mouseover event that displays the number above each point. This turns out to be much more difficult than it seemed at first. I want the numbers to display above each point as you mouse over it, but we have to read in the length of the number to display it correctly. I also found that when the points were close together it's hard to tell which point was selected. So now, when you mouseover it doubles the size of the point.
